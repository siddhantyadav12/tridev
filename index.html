About Us

Nestled in the serene hills of Pyuthan, Tridev Palace offers a perfect blend of traditional Nepali hospitality and modern comfort. Our family-owned hotel has been welcoming guests for over a decade with warm service and breathtaking mountain views.

Whether you're here for business, a family vacation, or a peaceful retreat, we strive to make every stay memorable. Experience authentic local culture while enjoying world-class amenities.
